# Prompts used to generate slop counterparts

## Slop A (counterpart to "The Last Signal")
"Write a blog post about how internet trust signals are dying. Cover how SEO, reviews, social media, phone calls, and email have all been degraded by spam and AI. Discuss what might replace them."

## Slop B (counterpart to "Keywords Are Tiny Circles")
"Write a blog post about how keyword-based advertising is outdated and how embedding-based or semantic advertising could replace it. Explain why the transition is difficult and what it would look like."
